Emergency Drinking Water

Access to potable drinking water can be a game-changer in emergency situations. Keep a safe, triple-purified supply within reach with our easy-to-open pouches.

  • Approved by USCG, Lloyd’s Register, UKMCA, New Zealand
  • Drop tested to 36 meters
  • 5-year shelf life
  • Water is triple purified: reverse osmosis, UV sterilized and ozonized
  • Withstands temperature range of -22ºF to +149ºF
  • Single-serve packaging makes it easier and more effective to ration during emergencies
  • Can be used as single serving or overwrapped in multiple combinations
  • Easy to store in small places, such as an auto glove compartment
  • Easy to open
  • Average burst strength of a filled pouch 862 ft. lbs., best in industry
